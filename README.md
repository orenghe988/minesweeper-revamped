### ❗IMPORTANT: This started as a school project where using vanilla JS (and other vanilla tools) was required. The app will be reworked to take advantage of Next.JS (React) and more tools.

---

### **A complete rework** of the old-ayout minesweeper, with a refreshed look and feel, and two brand new game modes.
- **Fences mode (PENDING):** Grants players the ability to craft a limited number of fences around game squares, in order to isolate an area on the board that might be a source to an explosion.

- **Unstable mines mode (PENDING):** Mines have a small chance of exploding, with 5 seconds notice. The player will have to immediately place fences in order to isolate that square from the rest of the board.
